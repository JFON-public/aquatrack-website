    • AquaTrack Privacy Policy

    Last Updated: April 25, 2025

    Introduction

    AquaTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AquaTrack mobile application.

    Information We Collect

    Personal Data: AquaTrack collects the following information to provide personalized hydration recommendations:

    • Gender
    • Weight
    • Activity level
    • Drinking habits and preferences
    • Beverage consumption records
    • Reminder settings

    Weather Data: With your permission, we may access local weather information to adjust hydration recommendations.

    How We Use Your Information

    We use the information we collect to:

    • Provide personalized hydration recommendations
    • Track your progress toward hydration goals
    • Send notifications and reminders if enabled
    • Improve our application's features and user experience

    Data Storage and Security

    AquaTrack stores all your personal data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not transmit your personal data to our servers or third parties.

    Third-Party Services

    AquaTrack may use Apple's WeatherKit to access weather information. This information is processed according to Apple's privacy standards.

    Children's Privacy

    AquaTrack is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that data.

    Your Rights

    You can access, edit, or delete your data at any time through the app's settings. If you choose to delete the app, all associated data will be removed from your device.

    Consent

    By using AquaTrack, you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@aquatrack.app.
